Zoe West

"All of the Lights" is about creating a space where comfort meets celebration, where every corner invites friendly conversations, and where the spirit of the season is found in shared laughter and warm greetings. Join us for an evening that celebrates the simple pleasure of good company and heartfelt connections. Warm your hands with steaming mugs of hot chocolate or coffee, enjoy some homemade Christmas treats, and get preoccupied with some activities planned for the evening like candlemaking, christmas decoration crafting and more. We will wrap the evening up with some christmas carols.

How you
can participate

You're welcome to join in the organizing team, to help with the preparations, to bring your own ideas and to join in the fun. You can also bring your own christmas treats to share, or come earlier to help in setting up.
